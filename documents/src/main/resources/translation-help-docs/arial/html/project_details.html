Project details

The right panel displays project details in different tabs, while the left panel displays a tree view of your project and its components. You may access to them by selecting the tab of your interest or by clicking the icon on the left.

Every new project is displayed under the Translation Projects folder on the left. In order to create your translation worksets, their characteristics must be previously defined, i.e., any exclusion refsets or linked refsets, the source language(s) and the target language. These may be displayed by clicking the respective tabs on the right panel and will be individually described.

Exclusion Refsets tab: In the Exclusion RefSet Tab you may define any group of concepts you may want to use as exclusions in worksets of this project. Adding them here only creates a shortcut to the refsets, the content will not be automatically excluded from the worksets, that decision is made in a workset by workset basis.

In order to specify an exclusion refset, follow the steps described below:
  • Open the taxonomy tab,
  • select the refset you want to exclude from your translation project,
  • drag & drop it into the Exclusion refset panel on the right,
  • press “Save”

Linked Refsets tab: This is a refset repository where refsets related with the project may be kept ‘at hand’

Linked refset window as described for the Exclusion Refsets.

Source and Target Languages: More than one source language may be specified; however, only one target language is allowed, as the translation tool allows performing one translation at a time for a given source language(s). If you want to change the selected target language, you should just drag and drop the newly selected target language and it will be overwritten in the target language window.
  • Open the taxonomy tab,
  • select the language refset(s) you will specify as your source language (more than one may be selected),
  • drag & drop it into the Source language panel on the right.
  • Again on the taxonomy tab, select the language refset you will choose as your target language.
  • Drag and drop it in the target language field at the bottom of the right panel.
  • Press “Save”
