Creation of projects

  • Open the Translation Workbench and select 'Open' --> 'Project Manager' from the upper left menu. The project manager tab will be displayed:

  • The project managment screen will be opened with a left panel displaying a tree view and a right panel with tabs showing the project details:

Specifying project features

  • Click on the ‘Add project’ button to create a new project:

  • A new window is displayed requesting to enter a new project name. Enter a new project name and click “enter”:

  • The new project is displayed under the Translation Projects folder on the left.  In order to create your translation worksets, their characteristics must be previously defined, i.e., any exclusion refsets or linked refsets, the source language(s) and the target language. These may be displayed by clicking the respective tabs on the right pane and will be individually described.

Exclusion Refsets tab:In the Exclusion RefSet Tab you may define any group of concepts you may want to use as exclusions in worksets of this project. Adding them here only creates a shortcut to the refsets, the content will not be automatically excluded from the worksets, that decision is made in a workset by workset basis.

  • Open the taxonomy tab, select the refset you want to exclude from your translation project, drag & drop it into the Exclusion refset panel on the right, and press “Save”.
  • In case you want to delete a refset from your exclusion list, you may select the refset you want to delete and type ‘d’ or right click on the selected refset to remove it. You may also click the “Remove Selected RefSets” button.

  • Your selection will be saved upon clicking the ‘Save’ button.
  • A message is displayed when your specifications have been saved:

Linked Refsets tab: This is a refset repository where refsets related with the project may be kept ‘at hand’, to be translated now or in the future.

  • Open the taxonomy tab, select the refset you want to link to your translation project, drag & drop it into the Linked refset panel on the right, and press “Save”.
  • In case you want to delete a refset from your linked refset list, type ‘d’ or right click on the selected refset to remove it. You may also click the “Remove Selected RefSets” button.

  • Your selection will be saved upon clicking ‘Save’ button.
  • A message is displayed when your specifications have been saved:

Source and Target Languages:

  • Open the taxonomy tab, select the language refset (s) you will specify as your source language (more than one may be selected),
  • drag & drop it into the Source language pane on the right.
  • Again on the taxonomy tab, select the language refset you will choose as your target language.
  • Drag and drop it in the target language field at the bottom of the right panel.
  • Press “Save”.
  • You may type ‘d’ (or right click on the selected refset or click "Remove selected refsets" button to delete any refset from your language specification.

  • More than one source language may be specified, while only one target language is allowed, as the translation tool allows performing one translation at a time for a given source language(s).
  • Example: English and Swedish may be used as source languages for translation into Danish, but it is not possible to translate from English into Spanish and Portuguese at the same time.

Source languages refsets may be removed from the specifications either by:

(a) Selecting them and clicking the ‘Remove selected refsets’ button:

(b) Right clicking on the selected  language to be removed:

(c) Select a language to be removed and type “d”.

  • The target language refset may be changed by dragging & dropping another language refset into the Target language window (notice that it cannot be removed but it is replaced by another target language).

  • Likewise, if an invalid concept is selected as target language, a message will appear and the concept will not be dragged into the Target language window:
