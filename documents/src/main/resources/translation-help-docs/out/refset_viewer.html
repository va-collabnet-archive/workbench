Refset viewer

The refset viewer allows seeing the members of a given refset.

When this tool is selected, the refset viewer is displayed in the lower third of the screen:

Select a refset, drag and drop it into the refset title box:

The refset members are displayed when the selected refset is dragged into the refset viewer window. Also, the total number of refset members are displayed in brackets:

The window may be expanded/reduced by clicking the expand/reduce icons.
