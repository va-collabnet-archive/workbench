Editor mode

  • Full editor: allows any kind of translation work, adding or modifying FSNs, preferred terms or synonyms
  • Preferred term editor: enters automatically into preferred term editing mode, and all other functions are disabled.
  • Synonyms editor: enters automatically into synonyms editor mode, and all other functions are disabled.

NOTE: for this and all windows in this function, your specifications will be applied upon clicking “Apply” button. Once you have specified all your preferences, you must click Accept button.
